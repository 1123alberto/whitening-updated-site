# SEO Audit & Quality Rater Assessment: i-smile.gr (2026 Update)

**Audit Subject:** i-smile.gr (Cosmetic Dentistry - Dentplant Clinic)  
**Location:** Palaio Faliro, Greece  
**Audit Date:** April 2026  
**Perspective:** Google Search Quality Rater & SEO Technical Lead  

---

## 1. Executive Summary
The 2026 update for **i-smile.gr** represents a significant shift from a specialized whitening landing page to a professional cosmetic dentistry authority (Dentplant Clinic). The move to **Greek-as-default** is a high-impact decision for local search rankings. However, to achieve "Best-in-Class" status, the site must address specific YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) trust signals and technical bilingual mapping.

---

## 2. E-E-A-T Assessment (Quality Rater Standards)
Google evaluates medical sites with the highest level of scrutiny.

### **Expertise & Experience (High)**
* **Strengths:** Mention of advanced systems (eMax, Empress) and association with the "Dentplant Clinic" brand.
* **Gap:** The site lacks a dedicated "Our Doctors" page with individual biographies. Google's Search Quality Raters (SQRG) look for a named human expert responsible for the medical advice/treatments.

### **Authoritativeness (Medium-High)**
* **Strengths:** Strong focus on specific treatments like veneers and professional whitening.
* **Action:** Inclusion of professional affiliations (e.g., Hellenic Dental Association) in the footer would solidify this score.

### **Trustworthiness (Needs Improvement)**
* **Requirement:** As a YMYL site, the "About Us" and "Contact" pages must provide high transparency.
* **Missing Elements:** Clear legal info (AFM/VAT number), GDPR/Privacy Policy (required for EU compliance and trust), and patient testimonials that link to verified sources (Google Maps/Facebook).

---

## 3. Bilingual SEO Strategy
Making Greek the default language is the correct move for the Palaio Faliro market.

### **Technical Recommendations:**
1. **Hreflang Tags (Critical):** Implement the following in the `<head>` of all pages to prevent duplicate content penalties:
   - `<link rel="alternate" hreflang="el" href="https://i-smile.gr/" />`
   - `<link rel="alternate" hreflang="en" href="https://i-smile.gr/en/" />`
2. **Translation Quality:** Avoid direct machine translation for medical terms. The current Greek copy should be audited for "Natural Language" flow to pass Google's "Helpful Content" checks.
3. **Internal Linking:** Ensure Greek pages link only to Greek pages. Breaking the language flow during a user session is a negative UX signal.

## 5. Local SEO & Google Business Profile (GBP)
Since the physical location is in **Palaio Faliro**, the site must sync perfectly with local signals.

* **NAP Consistency:** Name, Address, and Phone must be identical on the website footer, the "Contact" page, and the Google Business Profile.
* **Google Maps Integration:** Embed a live Google Map on the "Contact" page.
* **Keyword Focus:** Target hyper-local keywords:
  - *Λεύκανση δοντιών Παλαιό Φάληρο*
  - *Όψεις πορσελάνης Νότια Προάστια*
  - *Cosmetic Dentist Athens* (for the English version)
